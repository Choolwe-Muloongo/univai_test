# **App Name**: UnivAI

## Core Features:

- Personalized Study Plan Generation: AI tool generates personalized study plans based on the student's learning history and goals.
- AI Tutoring: AI tool answers student questions instantly.
- Course Content Delivery: Display courses and lessons with multimedia content.
- Online Exams: Conduct online exams with randomized question banks.
- Progress Tracking Dashboard: Allow students to track progress visually on their dashboards.
- Peer-to-Peer Networking: Facilitate in-app messaging and discussion groups for students.
- Job Board: Enable students to apply for internships and jobs.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Gold (#FFD700) to represent the richness of knowledge and opportunity, evoking a sense of value and excellence.
- Background color: A desaturated, dark green (#1A3320) creates a professional, secure learning environment.
- Accent color: Red-orange (#FF4500) will provide calls to action.
- Font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font that gives a modern, machined, objective look, and works for headlines and body text.
- Modern and clean design to promote easy navigation.
- Use relevant, visually consistent icons.
- Subtle animations to enhance user engagement and highlight key features.
